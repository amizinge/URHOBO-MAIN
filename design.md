# UAPR Website Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep earth tones - rich terracotta (#B85450), warm ochre (#CC8B3C)
- **Secondary**: Forest green (#2D5016), warm cream (#F5F1E8)
- **Accent**: Gold highlights (#D4AF37) for emphasis and cultural richness
- **Text**: Deep charcoal (#2C2C2C) for readability

### Typography
- **Display Font**: Playfair Display (serif) - for headings and cultural emphasis
- **Body Font**: Inter (sans-serif) - for modern readability
- **Accent Font**: Crimson Text (serif) - for quotes and cultural content

### Visual Language
- **Cultural Authenticity**: Incorporate traditional Urhobo patterns and motifs as subtle design elements
- **Heritage Elegance**: Sophisticated, museum-quality aesthetic that honors cultural significance
- **Modern Functionality**: Clean, accessible interface that serves all generations
- **Community Warmth**: Welcoming design that encourages participation and connection

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Smooth animations for interactive elements and page transitions
- **ECharts.js**: Data visualizations for family trees and demographic insights
- **Splide.js**: Image carousels for cultural galleries and testimonials
- **p5.js**: Generative background patterns inspired by Urhobo art
- **Typed.js**: Animated text effects for cultural quotes and welcome messages
- **Splitting.js**: Letter-by-letter animations for headings
- **Matter.js**: Physics-based interactions for family tree connections

### Animation Effects
- **Text Effects**: 
  - Typewriter animation for cultural proverbs and welcome messages
  - Color cycling emphasis on key headings
  - Split-by-letter stagger for section titles
- **Background**: 
  - Subtle particle system with traditional pattern motifs
  - Gentle aurora gradient flow in earth tones
- **Interactive Elements**:
  - Smooth hover transformations with 3D depth
  - Ken Burns effect on cultural images
  - Parallax scrolling for depth and engagement

### Header Effect
- **Hero Background**: Cinematic cultural imagery with subtle overlay animations
- **Navigation**: Fixed header with smooth transitions and cultural pattern accents
- **Welcome Text**: Large, impactful typography with typewriter and color effects

### Scroll Motion
- **Reveal Animations**: Content slides up with 20px translation, 200ms duration
- **Stagger Effects**: Cards and elements appear with 50ms delays
- **Parallax**: Background images move at 0.5 speed for depth
- **Trigger Point**: Animations start when elements enter top 50% of viewport

### Hover Effects
- **Cards**: 3D tilt with subtle shadow expansion and gold accent borders
- **Images**: Zoom with overlay text reveal and cultural pattern masks
- **Buttons**: Color morphing from terracotta to gold with glow effects
- **Links**: Underline animation with traditional pattern reveals

### Cultural Integration
- **Pattern Overlays**: Subtle Urhobo textile patterns as background textures
- **Color Symbolism**: Earth tones representing connection to land and ancestry
- **Image Treatment**: Warm color grading that honors traditional aesthetics
- **Iconography**: Custom icons inspired by Urhobo cultural symbols

## Layout Principles
- **Grid System**: 12-column responsive grid with cultural proportion ratios
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace using golden ratio proportions
- **Hierarchy**: Clear visual hierarchy respecting cultural significance
- **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios and screen reader compatibility
